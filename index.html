Welcome to The Tea Veranda

Nestled in the heart of Clevedon, we bring you the finest premium Ceylon tea alongside classic homemade treats and fresh light lunches. Our space is designed to feel like a warm, welcoming escape — perfect for a break from exploring the pier, meeting friends, or a special occasion.


Every pot of tea is brewed with care, and all our cakes, scones, and savoury dishes are made fresh daily using quality ingredients.
