Welcome to The Tea Veranda

We are a charming family-run independent traditional English tearoom, located at 30 Hill Road, Clevedon BS21 7PH — just a short 20–30 minute drive from Bristol.

Our Ceylon Speciality

We are proud to specialise in authentic Premium Ceylon loose-leaf teas, sourced directly from the beautiful hill country of Sri Lanka — bringing you the finest, freshest flavours from these very tea fields.

Step inside to discover unique decor, delicate hanging florals, and sparkling chandeliers. We serve fresh scones, afternoon tea, light lunches, cakes, macarons and sweet treats.
